About Our University

Bangladesh Army University of Engineering & Technology is situated in Qadirabad Cantonment, Natore. Information Communication Engineering is one of the most outstanding department of BAUET. This department started it's journey on 14 March 2016 with only 6 students and 2 lecturers. Now it's a department of more than 150+ students and 10+ lectureres. This department offers great courses about programming, networking and communication.It was the visionary leadership of the Honorable Prime Minister of People’s Republic of Bangladesh Sheikh Hasina to establish a Technical Institute of Armed Forces. Accordingly, the Honorable Prime Minister, People’s Republic of Bangladesh, Sheikh Hasina unveiled the Foundation Plaque on 15 August 2014.
